How it works

Three steps to your forecast

No account needed to start. Your first forecast takes under two minutes.

01
Enter your details

Your age, current super balance, salary, planned retirement age, and how much you'd like to spend each year. Takes about 90 seconds.

02
We run the numbers

Harbour runs 1,000 simulations using your inputs plus current Centrelink rates, ATO super rules, and ASFA retirement standards.

03
See your results

Best, median, and worst-case projections. Your likely Age Pension. A plain English summary of what it all means for your retirement.

What you'll see

Real answers, not round numbers

Monte Carlo projection chart
Three outcome bands — optimistic, median, and pessimistic — plotted from today to age 90. See the range of realistic possibilities, not just a single number.
Super balance at retirement
The median projected balance when you stop working, based on your current super, salary, SG contributions, and compound growth.
Centrelink Age Pension estimate
Calculated using the assets test and income test (deeming rules). Shows your fortnightly entitlement — the way Centrelink reports it.
Age your money lasts to
Best, median, and worst case. Tells you how likely your savings are to last through retirement at your target spending level.
Plain English summary
No jargon. Just a clear paragraph explaining what your forecast means and whether you're on track for the retirement you have in mind.
